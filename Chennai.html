CHENNAI

Chennai: The Gateway to South India

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of Tamil Nadu, India. Located on the Coromandel Coast along the Bay of Bengal, Chennai boasts the second-longest beachfront in the world. With a rich history spanning over 370 years, Chennai has grown into a major hub for medical tourism, IT services, and cultural heritage.

Best Time to Visit Chennai

The best time to visit Chennai is between December and March when the temperature remains between 19°C to 28°C. Being a coastal city, Chennai experiences hot and humid weather throughout the year.

Top Attractions in Chennai

1. Marina Beach

Marina Beach stretches over 12 km, making it the second-longest beach in the world. It is a perfect spot for relaxing walks and enjoying street food.

2. Historic Temples

Chennai is home to several ancient and architecturally stunning temples:

  • Kapaleeswarar Temple: Dedicated to Lord Shiva with amazing Dravidian architecture.
  • Parthasarathy Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.

3. Famous Churches

  • Santhome Basilica: Built over the tomb of St. Thomas.
  • St. Mary’s Church: One of the oldest churches in India.

4. Theosophical Society

The international headquarters of the Theosophical Society is in Chennai. It has a 450-year-old banyan tree, one of the largest in India.

5. Marina Beach Road (Kamarajar Salai)

A drive or walk along this road offers a view of heritage buildings, including Chepauk Stadium and the University of Madras.

Things to Do in Chennai

  • Explore Local Cuisine: Try idli, dosa, filter coffee, and seafood.
  • Visit Fort St. George: Built in 1644, it houses a museum with colonial-era artifacts.
  • Shop at T. Nagar: Famous for Kanjivaram sarees and jewelry.
  • Attend Cultural Performances: December hosts the Margazhi Music Festival, showcasing Carnatic music and Bharatanatyam dance.

How to Reach Chennai?

By Train

  • Chennai Central Railway Station: 3 km from the city center.
  • Chennai Egmore Railway Station: 2 km from the city center.

By Bus

The Chennai Mofussil Bus Terminus (CMBT) provides bus services to cities like Pondicherry (151 km) and Tirupati (133 km).

By Air

The Chennai International Airport is 14 km from the city center and connects to domestic and international destinations.
