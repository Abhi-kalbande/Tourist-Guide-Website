VARANASI

Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Benares, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a major spiritual hub in India.

Best Time to Visit Varanasi

The best time to visit Varanasi is between November and February when the weather is cool and ideal for sightseeing.

Places to Visit in Varanasi

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva and an important pilgrimage site.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

Famous for its grand Ganga Aarti ceremony held every evening, offering a spiritually uplifting experience.

Assi Ghat

A peaceful ghat where pilgrims take a holy dip, known for its cultural vibrancy.

Sarnath

Located outside Varanasi, Sarnath is where Buddha delivered his first sermon. Visit Dhamek Stupa, Mulagandha Kuti Vihar, and the Sarnath Museum.

Manikarnika Ghat

One of the main cremation ghats, holding immense spiritual significance.

Things to Do in Varanasi

Witness the Ganga Aarti

Attend the mesmerizing evening aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat.

Boat Ride on the Ganges

A sunrise or sunset boat ride along the river provides breathtaking views of the city.

Explore the Old City

Wander through the narrow, bustling lanes to experience the heritage and spirituality of Varanasi.

Visit Sarnath

Explore Buddhist ruins and learn about the city's historical significance.

Shop for Banarasi Silk

Varanasi is known for its exquisite silk sarees. Visit local markets to witness the craftsmanship.
