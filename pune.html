Pune Travel Guide

Are you planning to visit Pune? If you don’t know where to go or what to do, then you have come to the right place. In this guide, we will reveal the best places to visit in Pune.

Pune is renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and top-tier academic institutions, often referred to as the "Oxford of the East." The city boasts a unique blend of traditional and modern cultures, making it a dynamic and culturally rich destination.

Best Time to Visit Pune

The best time to visit Pune is during the monsoon season (June to September) when the city is lush and green. The pleasant weather and beautiful landscapes make sightseeing an enjoyable experience.

Best Places to Visit in Pune

  • Shaniwar Wada: The historical fort of the Maratha Peshwas, known for its grand architecture and beautiful gardens.
  • Aga Khan Palace: Built in 1892, this heritage site played a significant role in India's freedom struggle.
  • Sinhagad Fort: A popular trekking destination offering breathtaking views.
  • Shinde Chhatri: A memorial dedicated to Mahadji Shinde, featuring intricate architecture.
  • Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum: Showcasing a vast collection of Indian artifacts, sculptures, and musical instruments.
  • Pataleshwar Cave Temple: An 8th-century rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Osho Ashram: A meditation resort for spiritual seekers.
  • Vetal Tekdi: A hill offering panoramic city views and great hiking spots.
  • Katraj Snake Park and Zoo: A great place for families to learn about wildlife conservation.
  • Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple: One of Pune’s most famous temples, attracting devotees year-round.

Things to Do in Pune

  • Take a Nature Walk: Visit serene locations like Osho Teerth Park, Empress Garden, and Pashan Lake.
  • Explore the Local Cuisine: Try local delights like vada pav, misal pav, and bhakri.
  • Attend Cultural Events: Experience live performances at Bal Gandharva Rang Mandir and Nehru Memorial Hall.
  • Shopping: Explore FC Road, MG Road, and Pune Central Mall for local and international shopping experiences.
  • Outdoor Activities: Enjoy trekking, cycling, paragliding, and rafting in and around Pune.
